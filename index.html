How It Works

Five steps, one shelf-worthy object.

01

You send a photo

A drawing, a doodle, a reference photo — whatever you want turned into an object. Email or WhatsApp it over, with a sense of size and any details that matter to you.

02

It gets modeled in 3D

Your 2D image is rebuilt as a digital 3D model, keeping the shapes and character of the original drawing.

03

We print it

The model is 3D printed at the size that suits the piece — same proportions, now with real depth and weight.

04

My friend hand-paints it

Every print is painted by hand, not airbrushed from a template — so the final color and finish are one-of-a-kind, matched to your original image.

05

It ships to you

Packed carefully and sent your way — from your sketch to your shelf.
